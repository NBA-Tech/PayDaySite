Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our movie investment platform and how screenplay-based investments work.

What is the purpose of this platform?

Our platform allows users to invest in curated movie screenplays. As these movies develop through production, investors earn returns based on progress and performance.

What does it mean to invest in a screenplay?

You’re investing in the early stages of a film’s life — the story. Based on potential, creator credibility, and market interest, your investment grows as the movie evolves.

When do movie investment opportunities open?

Each screenplay is launched with a set investment window. You'll be notified when a new movie is available through the app or email alerts.

How can I start investing in movie?

To participate, create a free account, verify your identity (KYC), and fund your wallet. Once set up, you can explore screenplays and invest directly through your dashboard.
